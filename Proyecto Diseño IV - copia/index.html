about us

On the southernmost tip of Roatán Island, Honduras, overlooking West Bay Beach, Grand Roatán Caribbean Resort is a rare tropical gem that merges authenticity with unmatched luxury and elegance. Stylish and sophisticated, our resort hotel is nestled between a pristine forest sanctuary and the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean. Guests enjoy 48 oceanfront or ocean view suites with unparalleled beach sightlines and wade-in access to the Mesoamerican Reef — the world's second largest barrier reef — just 30 yards offshore. With its breathtaking coastline, luxury suites and residences for sale, five-star amenities and a sense of solitude that blends perfectly with its natural surroundings, Grand Roatán Resort welcomes you to enjoy the quintessential Caribbean lifestyle for a week or perhaps even a lifetime.​

our services

FUN THINGS TO DO IN GRAND ROATAN Grand Roatán Caribbean Resort is one of the best-located hotels for enjoying everything there is to do on Roatán Island. Whether you’re seeking a world-class dive resort, a family friendly hotel or a spot for your romantic getaway, we’re the perfect choice. Exhilarating recreational opportunities – from one of the most magnificent beaches in the Caribbean and the second largest barrier reef on earth to world-class fishing, scuba diving and snorkeling to the shops, restaurants and nightlife of West End — await you along the southern coast of Roatán. In addition to the breathtaking natural beauty found throughout the island, choose from a diverse range of adventures, activities and exciting things to do in Roatán.
